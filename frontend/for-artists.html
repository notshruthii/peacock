Export Compliance & Guidelines

Ensure your handicrafts are ready for international markets with our step-by-step guidance.

Product Quality

Inspect each product for defects, durability, and finish. Ensure materials meet safety standards and non-toxic requirements.

Packaging & Labeling

Use sturdy packaging and include labels with product name, material, artisan info, and country of origin.

Documentation

Prepare invoices, export forms, and customs declarations properly. Keep records for audits and shipments.

Testing & Certification

Test fragile or wearable products for safety. Obtain certifications for regulated markets to ensure compliance.
